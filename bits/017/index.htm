babel fish tower 1
Technically speaking, this sketch explores text sliding on a helix.

6 different lines of text are sliding in parallel, each of them starting at the beginning of a turn.

The line at the bottom contains some original text in french. The lines following are successive translations (to english, then to german, then all over again) made with a robot at babelfish.
