paper box 1
Three sliders and one scrollbar interacting together...

In response to the lack of user-interface components in the processing environment, the Slider and ScrollBar widgets (influenced by their Java Swing counterparts) were created from scratch with the purpose of being used in the numerous type experiments to follow.
