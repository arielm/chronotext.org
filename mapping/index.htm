Mapping, augmented

A study on the aesthetics of mapping in the context of augmented reality, cooked with three major ingredients:

  • • Flickr: outstanding source of photographic material, by folks / for folks.
  • • Camera matching: the art of turning pictures into 3d spaces.
  • • Real-time 3d flow of text and textures over surfaces.

Ho Tokyo, Tokyo

Camouflage texture coupled with biblical oracle on the siege of Jerusalem (Isaiah 29:1-8), flowing over the facade of a Bauhaus style building, presumably in Tokyo...

control

  • • Zoom in-and-out with the mouse-wheel.
  • • Drag with the mouse to pan inside the picture.
  • • Press the space-bar to reveal the underlying photo.
The original photo, as found on Kimi Iwasaki's flickr photostream.
