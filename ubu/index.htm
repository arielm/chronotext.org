UBU/UI
a pure exercice de style

Exercises in Style, written by Raymond Queneau, is a collection of 99 retellings of the same story, each in a different style.

UBU/UI is my first attempt to retell another story: proposing an alternative interface to ubuweb:sound entry page. It's not necessarily better than the original, but more like a variation. I still have 98 tries left to enhance it…

The style used in this variation: black and red typewriter text à la Carl Andre. Talmud like layout, with commentary on the left. Flowing content à la chronotext. Slight rotation à la Arte logo.
