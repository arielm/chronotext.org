Saving forgotten chronotext experiments from digital extinction

Created between 2003 and 2010, these sketches stopped working a few years ago when the Java technology became obsolete in the browser.

They have been remastered thanks to emerging technologies such as Emscripten and WebGL and they're now back to the web!
